Add2Calendar - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Add2Calendar collects the following information:

  • Your Google account email address
  • Access to your Google Calendar (with your explicit permission)
  • Timetable images you upload for processing

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Create calendar events based on your uploaded timetable
  • Identify which calendar to add events to
  • Improve our timetable parsing algorithm

3. Data Storage

Add2Calendar does not permanently store your timetable images or calendar data. Images are processed and then deleted from our servers. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke calendar access at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Request deletion of any data we may have by contacting us
